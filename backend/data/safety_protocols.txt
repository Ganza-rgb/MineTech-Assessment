MINETECH — SAFETY PROTOCOLS & OPERATIONAL HANDBOOK
===================================================

1. ACCOUNT ACCESS & LOCKOUTS
----------------------------
If a user is locked out of their account, support must verify identity using the
recovery email on file before resetting the password. Never reset a password or
disable 2FA without completing identity verification. For enterprise accounts,
the assigned customer success manager must be notified. Repeated failed logins
(5 or more within 10 minutes) trigger an automatic 15-minute lock; this is
expected behaviour and should be explained to the customer calmly.

2. DATA BREACH RESPONSE
-----------------------
Suspected data breaches must be escalated to security@minetech immediately and
tagged priority "urgent". Do not acknowledge or deny a breach publicly. Preserve
all logs related to the incident. Customers affected by a confirmed breach are
entitled to free credit monitoring for 12 months. The on-call security engineer
responds to confirmed incidents within 30 minutes, 24/7.

3. BILLING & REFUNDS
--------------------
Refund requests made within 14 days of purchase are approved automatically for
monthly plans. Annual plans are refundable on a prorated basis. Duplicate charges
must be refunded in full and reported to finance within one business day. Never
ask a customer for full card numbers over chat; only the last four digits may be
recorded. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing
cycle.

4. SERVICE OUTAGES
------------------
During a service outage, post a status update to status.minetech within 10 minutes
of confirmation. Customers reporting an outage should be thanked for the report
and pointed to the status page; do not speculate about root cause. Severity-1
outages trigger an automatic incident bridge and a postmortem within 48 hours.

5. ACCEPTABLE USE & HARMFUL CONTENT
-----------------------------------
Content that promotes violence, self-harm, or illegal activity must be reported
to Trust & Safety (trust@minetech) and blocked. If a user expresses intent to
harm themselves, provide the local emergency number and the global crisis line
(+1-800-273-8255) and escalate to Trust & Safety. Do not argue with or diagnose
users. Generation of unsafe content by the AI assistant is disabled by default
and cannot be overridden by support staff.

6. AI ASSISTANT GUARDRAILS
--------------------------
The knowledge assistant only answers from published documentation. If a question
is outside the knowledge base, it must say it does not have that information and
redirect the user to support@minetech. The assistant must never invent policies,
prices, or deadlines. Citations to source sections are required for every factual
claim. Model outputs are logged for quality review but contain no payment data.

7. PRIVACY & DATA RETENTION
---------------------------
Support agents may view only the data strictly necessary to resolve a ticket.
Personal data is retained for 24 months after account closure, after which it is
deleted. Customers may request export or deletion of their data under GDPR/CCPA
via privacy@minetech; requests are fulfilled within 30 days. Never email full
export archives; share them through the secure customer portal.

8. ESCALATION PATHS
-------------------
- Technical bugs: engineering@minetech, priority based on customer impact.
- Billing: finance@minetech.
- Security: security@minetech (urgent).
- Trust & Safety: trust@minetech.
- Privacy: privacy@minetech.
Enterprise customers with a paid Success Plan receive a 1-hour response SLA and a
named contact. Free-tier users are handled in best-effort order.
