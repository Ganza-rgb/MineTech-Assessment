# MineTech Technical FAQ

## Account & Password

### How do I reset my password?
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Create a new password (must be 8+ characters with mixed case and numbers)
6. Log in with your new password

### My account is locked - what do I do?
1. Wait 15 minutes and try again
2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Contact support@minetech.com with your registered email

### How do I update my profile?
Go to Settings > Profile and update your details.

## Billing

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, bank transfers, and mobile money.

### How do I request a refund?
Email billing@minetech.com with your order number. Refunds processed within 7 business days.

### Where can I find my invoices?
Go to Settings > Billing > Invoices

## Technical Support

### The app is running slow
1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Restart the application
5. Contact support if issue persists

### I'm getting an error message
1. Take a screenshot of the error
2. Note the time it happened
3. Try restarting the app
4. Submit a ticket with details

### How do I report a bug?
Go to Help > Report an Issue and fill out the form.

## Safety Protocols

### What should I do in an emergency?
1. Ensure personal safety first
2. Alert your supervisor immediately
3. Follow emergency evacuation procedures
4. Call the emergency hotline
5. Document the incident

### How often are safety drills conducted?
Monthly - all staff must participate.

### Where are emergency exits located?
Check the safety map in the lobby and break areas.
