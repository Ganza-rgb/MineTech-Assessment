# MineTech Rwanda — Company Overview

## 1. Mission & Vision

**MineTech Rwanda** is a pioneering deep-tech startup dedicated to transforming the African mining sector through intelligent, AI-powered digital infrastructure. Founded in 2019 by Rwandan tech entrepreneur **Kelvin Rwihimba**, the company digitizes traditional, paper-based mine workflows to dramatically enhance worker safety, maximize operational efficiency, and automate regulatory compliance.

**Headquarters:** Kigali, Rwanda  
**Founded:** 2019  
**Founder & CEO:** Kelvin Rwihimba  
**Sector:** Mining Technology / Deep Tech  
**Focus:** African mining operations (Rwanda and pan-African expansion)

## 2. Core Purpose

Making Mining Intelligence Accessible.

We enable efficient mining operations by helping our clients transition from paper-based systems to integrated digital platforms that balance safety, compliance, productivity, and profitability.

**Key statistic:** 80% of African mining operations still rely on paper-based systems, creating gaps in safety, compliance, and operational efficiency.

## 3. Approach

We partner with mining operations to drive operational excellence and sustainable growth. Our reputation is built on:
- **Affordability** — solutions 5–75× more affordable than enterprise alternatives
- **Technical robustness** — built for harsh African mining environments
- **Collaborative partnerships** — from strategy through to on-the-ground deployment, training, and ongoing optimization

## 4. Impact to Date

Since founding MineTech, we've built an integrated platform covering **15 operational departments** — from exploration and grade control to safety monitoring and financial operations.

- **150+** local mining cooperatives on waitlist
- **UNDP timbuktoo MineTech Accelerator Programme** participant
- Pan-African recognition for youth-led tech disruptions

## 5. Regulatory Context

MineTech Rwanda operates under the oversight of the **Rwanda Mines, Petroleum and Gas Board (RMB)**. All solutions are designed to meet:
- Rwandan mining regulatory requirements
- International mineral due diligence standards
- Worker safety compliance (RSSB — Rwanda Social Security Board)
