# MineTech Rwanda — Site Sensor & Safety Protocol (v2.0)
Document ID: MT-OPS-SAF-2026

## 1. Environmental Telemetry Nodes
- **Sensor Error Code: ERR-902 (Ventilation Drop):** Indicates gas accumulation or airflow drop below safety thresholds. The shift supervisor must evacuate the immediate shaft location within 3 minutes of alert.
- **Sensor Error Code: ERR-104 (Structural Strain):** Micro-fracture alert. Restrict all heavy machinery deployment within a 50-meter radius instantly.

## 2. Emergency Site Coverage & Clearance
- Any emergency crew reassignment or extended shift allocation requires continuous validation against the active worker database to ensure full Rwanda Social Security Board (RSSB) regulatory compliance automation.
